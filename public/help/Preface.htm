Preface

Welcome to LRN Catalyst, an education and engagement solution that inspires continuous, values-based decision-making in a streamlined, easy-to-use-and-administer online experience.

This user guide describes step-by-step how to perform basic tasks, such as taking a course, and changing your password. It also walks you through LRN Catalyst's various "social" features, such as rating and reviewing a course, and sharing a snippet of text to your social network.
