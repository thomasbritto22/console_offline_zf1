Reset a forgotten password

  1. Click forgot password? on the Sign In page.

  2. Enter your user name and/or e-mail, depending on your site's requirements.

  3. Click Reset My Password.

  4. The system will send you an e-mail with a temporary link. Click the link.

  5. At the prompt, enter your user name and new password (twice).

  6. Once your user name is validated, the system updates your password, and the temporary link is disabled.

  7. You are returned to the Sign In page. Enter the new password.

  8. After receiving a validation message, click the prompt to return to the My Profile page.
