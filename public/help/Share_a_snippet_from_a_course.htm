Share a snippet from a course

Many LRN Catalyst courses offer the ability to highlight text from the course and share it with othersâ€”via e-mail or a social network.

  1. (Optional) Highlight the text you want to share.

  2. Click the Share button at the bottom of the course window.
    A widget containing sharing destinations appears.

  3. Select a destination, either E-mailYour e-mail client opens, and if you've highlighted any text, it automatically appears within the message. You can then add your own comments, select one or more recipients, then send the message. or ChatterYour experience will depend on whether or not you're already signed in to Chatter. If not, you're prompted to sign in; you're then presented with the Chatter text entry form. If yes, you're immediately presented with the Chatter text entry form..

  4. If you've highlighted any text, it automatically appears within the text entry field.

  5. (Chatter only) Select a Chatter group where you want your message to appear.

  6. Add your own comments.

  7. Post your message.
