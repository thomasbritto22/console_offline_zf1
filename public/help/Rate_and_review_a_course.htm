Rate and review a course

  1. Complete the course.
    The Module Reviews page appears.

  2. Rate the course according to the listed criteria by clicking the stars.

  3. Write a review in the text box provided.

  4. Click Publish.
    Your ratings and review appear at the top of the list of reviews at the left.

More...

Ratings cover these four criteria:

  • Overall

  • Design

  • Course Writing

  • Relevance

All users within your enterprise will be able to see each other's ratings and reviews. Additionally, ratings are aggregated and averaged across the enterprise for each of these four criteria.
