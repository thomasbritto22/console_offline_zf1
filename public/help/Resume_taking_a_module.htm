Resume taking a module

LRN Catalyst lets you interrupt taking a module and resume later on.

To stop a module

  1. Click the X at the upper right of the module window.
    The module closes.

To resume a module

  1. Click My Queue.
    The My Queue page appears.

  2. Click the module that you want to resume.
    The Module Overview page appears.

  3. Click the Resume button.
    You're taken to the beginning of the section where you stopped.
